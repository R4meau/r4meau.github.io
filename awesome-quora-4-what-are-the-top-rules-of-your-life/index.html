Awesome Quora #4: What are the top rules of your life?

Written on
12-09-2019
awesome-quora, quora, quora-answers

Preamble

I’ve been actively reading Quora answers since July this year (2019).

Actively, as in, at least 10 questions and their top answers a day.

On an average, I bookmark 2 answers a day. I bookmark the ones that make the most impact on me during the day.

I have a lot of them waiting to be re-read in my Bookmarks section of Quora. There are so many of them that it scares the shit out of me.

So in the spirit of redistributing and having an easier access to my favorite ones, I decided to publicly post them on here, at least, just the best of them, according to me… on a daily basis.

I was scrolling down my Bookmarks to randomly pick the question and answer for today but when I read the beginning of today’s answer, it made me stop and think. My mom called me today, and as usual it didn’t go well because I’m holding grudges. I’m ashamed.

What are the top rules of your life?

The following answer is by Sara Berrada to this particular question.

9Psychological rules

  1. Take care of your parents. Never blame them for anything. Even if they were hard with you, it’s just because they want your best.
  2. Never blame yourself for past mistakes. It is what it is. Learn from them and promise yourself that you will never repeat them again.
  3. Be strong to let go of people who don’t care about your presence. I am talking here about your ex. Yes, if he or she dumped you, never respond to their calls or let them play with you again. Your pride must be your first occupation.
  4. Love is a beautiful feeling, but it’s not everything. You should first be happy alone.
  5. Learn to be flexible. And be ready for everything. People who you hang up with now may leave you at any given occasion.
  6. Always call your old friends. They remember the old version of you, which you are missing now.
  7. Never trust people completely. But you should find someone to whom you can tell everything.
  8. Believe in yourself no matter what. You are the only one who knows your own battle. So don’t give up.
  9. Don’t take everything seriously. Learn to ignore those who want to put you down. They are either jealous or extremely jealous. Because, logically people don’t make effort on people if they are not a threat for them.
  10. Don’t be so available for people. Be mysterious and don’t tell everything about you, especially your private life. They will always want to know more about you.
  11. Learn to manage your stress.
  12. Don’t tell your plans to anyone. The mind will act like you already achieved them.
  13. Do good to others and don’t expect anything in return. Good things will chase after you unexpectedly.
  14. Be kind especially with people in need. Those who are abased with others. Even a smile on their faces can change your day and theirs.

The above answer is by Sara Berrada to this particular question.

Side Notes

This is my first time adding this section to this series’ articles. I’m not always gonna add it.

I didn’t think it would be so difficult to forgive your parents. It’s like a mental war, no doubt. Could be just me.

To whomever found their way to my blog, my parents didn’t do anything harmful to me. At least, not physically and neither on purpose.

That’s the thing, I blame them for not being aware they were doing it to me.

And I realized I’m just being stupid. It’s like I expected them to know everything so they could be the perfect parents.

They are both the reason why I’m able to write this article right now. Also both the reason why I can love, be self-taught and become a tough man out there who can provide value.

So I forgive you mom and dad and I love you both very much.

I’ll call you more often. <3

Conclusion

I hope you enjoyed this answer as much as I did.

All I can say is I’m loving this series so far. It’s helping me find the answers that made an impact on me at a certain point in my life and most importantly, the ones that still do.

I really recommend you check out other answers to this question on Quora. I may have read it a long time ago and some new and even better answers may have popped up since.

If you don’t use Quora, feel free to leave a comment down below about this answer.
