A new way of blogging

Written on
07-02-2019
blogging, personal

So basically, no more external comments. This blog will only serve as public archive of my notes.

In the hopes that those particular notes will serve as an example for people who seek more out of life ;)

I want to write more openly and have more control over my content.

There will still be a comment section, and this comment section will contain updates from me, about the content of the article. This will also allow me to keep my articles short. I love this.

For example, if I’m training for a marathon and I ever decide to make it public, I’ll post about my improvements through the comment section.

People can still comment to contact me quickly :) I pre-moderate comments, so make sure to leave a way for me to get back to you.

Surely, the best way to get in touch with me is via email.

Peace.
